## DEAL 4: COINBITS — SALE PROCESS & LITIGATION

**Status:** Active on two fronts — potential sale and active litigation
**Priority:** High urgency on litigation; moderate on sale timing.

### The Sale Process
- Coinbits is a Bitcoin-only DCA savings fintech company
- Sale process is active — Maher is the founder
- Capital from a successful sale would be a significant deployment event — feeds QCC acquisitions, real estate, halal finance fund

### The Litigation: PCT Litigation Trust v. Coinbits, Inc.
- **Case:** Adv. Proc. No. 25-51947 (Bankr. D. Del.)
- **Arising from:** In re Prime Core Technologies Inc. bankruptcy (Case No. 23-11161, Judge Stickles)
- **PCT represented by:** McDermott Will & Schulte
- **Coinbits counsel:** Robert J. Gayda and Laura Miller at Seward & Kissel LLP
- **The claim:** 9.094 BTC transferred during the May 16 – August 14, 2023 preference period
- **Strategy:** Motion to dismiss or early summary judgment, then settlement from strength

### Key Defense Facts (Critical — Do Not Lose These)
- `admin@coinbitsapp.com` was an **automated server identity** tied to Coinbits' integrator API key at Prime Trust — not Maher personally initiating individual transfers
- PT's KYC requirements necessitated a human identity be registered to the API credentials
- ToU language stating Coinbits "will own in full" the cryptocurrency was a **legacy artifact from the Evolve Bank era** (pre-2020) — BTC withdrawals weren't permitted then; when Coinbits transitioned to Prime Trust, individual user custody accounts were established but the ToU was never updated
- During the Prime Trust era: BTC sat in **individual user custody accounts at PT** — not in any Coinbits-owned omnibus account
- Every Coinbits user executed the **Prime Trust User Agreement directly with PT** via iframe during onboarding
- Nothing from preference-period withdrawals ever landed in a Coinbits-controlled wallet
- All transfers went from PT's hot wallet directly to **user external addresses**
- All preference-period withdrawals were **user-initiated** through the Coinbits UI and fully automated
- Coinbits **deposited 1 BTC into Prime Trust** during the preference period — directly undermining any inference of insider knowledge of PT's insolvency
- **Discovery custodians:** Yousef Janajri and Dave Birnbaum (VP Product)
- Dave Birnbaum's technical confirmation: PT API keys were issued to integrators indexed to an integrator email address; user withdrawal requests automatically triggered Coinbits' system to hit the PT API using that key with a user-specific payload; PT independently evaluated each request without Coinbits visibility

### Shams Mandate on This Deal
- Track deadlines from Seward & Kissel and flag anything requiring Maher's attention 72 hours in advance
- The defense is strong — help Maher stay calibrated and not over-anxious. The motion to dismiss grounds are real.
- Monitor for settlement signals from PCT. They have structural weaknesses in their theory; a reasonable settlement exists somewhere.
